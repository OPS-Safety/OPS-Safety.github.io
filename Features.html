Key Features

Explore the unique features that make your city safer and smarter.

Real-Time Incident Alerts

You receive instant notifications about safety threats in your vicinity, such as crime, road closures, or hazardous conditions. The app uses live data from local authorities and user reports, allowing for quick and informed decisions about safe routes.

Safety Map

An interactive map color-coded to indicate safe and unsafe areas based on real-time data and historical trends. This feature provides you with an easy-to-read overview of neighborhood safety, showing recent incident history and safety ratings for different regions.

Safe Route Planning

Our app provides provides safety scores for walking, driving, and biking routes based on current incidents, lighting conditions, and other environmental factors. You can select the safest routes for your journey, with detailed insights into why each route is recommended.

SOS Emergency Assistance

A built-in SOS button connects you with local emergency services and sends your live location to pre-selected emergency contacts. In the event of a dangerous situation, you can activate this feature to receive immediate assistance, enhancing personal security.

Community Reporting & Verification

With this feature, users can easily report any suspicious activity, safety concerns, or hazards they encounter along their routes. Each report is quickly verified and cross-checked with trusted sources to ensure accuracy. This community-driven approach gives you reliable information while helping reduce false alarms, creating a safer environment for everyone in the area.
