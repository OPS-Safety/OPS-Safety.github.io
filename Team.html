About Our Product:

Our product, the uOttawa Safety Awareness Page, aims to enhance safety awareness around the University of Ottawa by providing real-time information on various types of hazards and risks in the surrounding area. The website combines informative reports, interactive maps, and categorization by severity and type of risk, allowing users to stay aware of their environment and take necessary precautions.
